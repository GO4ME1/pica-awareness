Understanding and Testing for Pica

What is Pica?

Pica is an eating disorder characterized by the persistent consumption of non-food items, such as dirt, chalk, or drywall. This condition can be harmful and is often associated with nutritional deficiencies or psychological issues.

Signs of Pica

  • Eating non-food items consistently
  • Experiencing health problems related to consumption
  • Seeking medical advice is essential

Testing for Pica

If you or someone you know exhibits signs of Pica, it's important to consult a healthcare professional for assessment and support.
